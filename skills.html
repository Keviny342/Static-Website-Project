My Skills

I have been playing the violin for about 2 years and is currently in Intimediate level orchestra. Which is the 2nd highest in our school. Playing a violin is harder then one might think. Unlike a piano when you press a key it plays the note for you clearly. On a violin you dont just brush the string with your bow. You have to have a perfect contact point between your bow and string in order to produce a clear sound.
